ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Serena, a fourth-year undergrad at the UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with an emphasis in human-computer interaction. This summer, I was an Associate Product Manager intern at Google working on machine learning algorithms for the Google Photos Assistant. I teamed up with PMs, designers, and engineers to develop new features which help users easily manage and declutter their photo library. Last spring, I co-directed a hip hop dance team and we performed a set about grief and loss. In my free time, I make duct tape hats and eat too much bread.

REVERSE POLAROID

Photos in this camera app pixelate with each moment displayed. The choice? Preserve the image by not viewing it, or view and let deteriorate. It's the personal Snapchat that tests the creative limits of counterfunctional design.
